# Welcome to BellSense

BellSense is a kettlebell training feedback system that provides real-time rep counting and quality scoring using a bell-mounted IMU sensor.

## What it tracks

- **Hardstyle swing** — the foundation of kettlebell training
- **Snatch** — the kettlebell sport staple
- **Clean** — power transfer movement
- **Goblet squat** — strength and stability
- **1-arm row** — posterior chain
- **OH press** — overhead strength
- **Turkish get-up** — stability and coordination
