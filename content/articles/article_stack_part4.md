# The BellSense Workout Stack — Part 4: Ballistics — The Main Event
*bellsense.app/articles/workout-stack-ballistics*

---

This is what you came for.

Swing. Snatch. Clean.

The heart of every BellSense session.

---

Ballistics are first in the stack for one reason.

Power expression requires a fresh nervous system.

You cannot generate maximal output on a tired CNS.

This is why the core work comes before — not after.

You arrive at this block primed, braced, and ready.

Now you work.

---

Every rep is a power expression.

Not a movement to complete. Not a box to check.

A deliberate, explosive, full-body effort.

Hip drive. Full tension. Lat engagement. Crisp lockout.

Every time.

---

This is where BellSense earns its keep.

Your velocity, your power output, your consistency across sets — all of it tracked, all of it honest.

You will see exactly when you're dialed in and exactly when you're starting to coast.

---

When the numbers drop — pause.

You know this by now.

Rest as needed. Come back loaded.

Accumulate quality reps, not junk.

---

A great ballistic set isn't the longest one.

It's the one where every rep looked like the first.

Same power. Same tension. Same drive.

That's the standard.

---

This block builds your engine.

Your conditioning, your power, your athletic capacity.

Everything else in the stack supports it.

---

*Show up fresh. Drive every rep. Leave nothing sloppy behind.*
