# BellSense Landing Page (MVP) – Developer Spec

## Goal
Convert cold traffic into:
- Hardware purchases
- Email captures (for pre-launch / waitlist)

Tone: Performance-driven, simple, no fluff. Speak to lifters, not general fitness.

---

## 1. HERO SECTION (Above the Fold)

**Layout:**
- Full-width image/video background (kettlebell swing in motion)
- Centered text + CTA

**Copy:**
Headline:
> Turn Your Kettlebell Into a Smart Training System

Subheadline:
> Track every rep, measure power, and see your performance improve in real time.

Primary CTA:
- Button: “Get Early Access” or “Buy Now”

Secondary CTA (optional):
- “See How It Works” (scroll anchor)

---

## 2. PROBLEM → SOLUTION

**Layout:**
- Two-column (Problem left, Solution right)

**Copy:**
Problem:
- You don’t know if your swings are improving
- You lose power across sets without realizing
- No feedback = wasted training

Solution:
> BellSense gives you objective feedback on every rep — automatically.

---

## 3. HOW IT WORKS (3 STEPS)

**Layout:**
- Horizontal 3-step cards

**Steps:**
1. Attach sensor to kettlebell
2. Perform swings (or other lifts)
3. View real-time metrics in the app

Keep each step short (1 sentence max)

---

## 4. CORE FEATURES

**Layout:**
- Grid (2x2 or stacked on mobile)

**Features:**

**Rep Counting**
- Near-zero false positives

**Velocity & Power**
- See how explosive each rep is

**Fatigue Tracking**
- Know when performance drops off

**Session Score**
- One number that reflects workout quality

---

## 5. SOCIAL PROOF / CREDIBILITY

**Layout:**
- Quotes or simple statements

Examples:
- “Built for serious training”
- “Designed by lifters, not influencers”

(Optional later: real testimonials)

---

## 6. USE CASES

**Layout:**
- 3 columns or stacked

**Segments:**
- Strength athletes
- Tactical / military
- General fitness users

Each gets 1–2 lines describing value

---

## 7. PRICING BLOCK

**Layout:**
- Centered pricing card

**Options:**

Option A:
- $120 – Device + Full App Access

Option B:
- $60 Device + $9.99/month (12-month minimum)

Include CTA button under each

---

## 8. FINAL CTA

**Layout:**
- Full-width banner

**Copy:**
> Train with data. Perform with intent.

Button:
- “Get Started”

---

## 9. FOOTER

Include:
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Contact Email
- Social links (optional)

---

## DESIGN NOTES

- Color scheme: Dark, high-contrast (black / charcoal / white text)
- Accent color: Bright (orange or electric blue)
- Typography: Bold, modern, athletic
- Spacing: Generous, minimal clutter

---

## TECH NOTES (MVP)

- Framework: Webflow, Framer, or simple React
- Mobile-first design
- Fast load time (compress images/video)
- Simple analytics (Google Analytics or similar)

---

## SUCCESS METRIC

- Conversion rate (visitor → email or purchase)
- Scroll depth (are users reaching pricing?)
