# BellSense Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

BellSense ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BellSense mobile
application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect,
use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after
changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how BellSense collects, uses, and protects
your data in connection with the use of the App and related hardware.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Account Information

-   Name\
-   Email address\
-   Login credentials

### B. Workout & Sensor Data

-   Repetition counts\
-   Exercise type\
-   Session duration\
-   Session timestamps\
-   Performance metrics derived from motion sensor (IMU) data\
-   Heart rate data (beats per minute, average, peak, and minimum) when
    a compatible Bluetooth heart rate monitor is connected

Raw continuous IMU streams are not stored. The App communicates with a
BLE-connected sensor device to receive motion data; no data is
transmitted from the App back to the hardware device.

### C. User Feedback Data

-   Subjective workout feel ratings (1–5 scale)\
-   Free-text notes provided by the user to describe or annotate
    sessions\
-   Manual rep count corrections submitted by the user

### D. Device & Diagnostic Data

-   Device model\
-   iOS version\
-   App version\
-   Crash reports\
-   Performance logs

### E. Analytics Data

-   Usage behavior\
-   Feature interaction\
-   Aggregated analytics data collected via Firebase services

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Services Used

BellSense uses Google Firebase for:

-   Authentication\
-   Cloud data storage\
-   Analytics\
-   Crash reporting

This includes Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. Data may be processed
on secure cloud infrastructure operated by Google.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

-   Provide workout tracking functionality\
-   Generate performance analytics\
-   Sync workout history across devices\
-   Improve rep detection, movement classification, and fatigue
    prediction accuracy using anonymized workout and session data\
-   Build and refine personalized movement baselines and fatigue
    profiles stored under your account\
-   Debug crashes and improve stability\
-   Communicate product updates\
-   Respond to user support inquiries

Session performance data (including rep metrics, velocity trends, fatigue
patterns, and subjective feel ratings) may be used in aggregate and
anonymized form to train and improve machine learning models that power
in-app features. This data is processed under your account and is not
shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

We do not sell personal information.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Data Storage

Workout and account data are stored locally on your device and in secure
cloud infrastructure. Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical
safeguards are implemented to protect your data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.\
Data may be shared with service providers strictly for hosting,
analytics, infrastructure support, or if required by law.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Beta Testing

During beta testing via TestFlight, Apple may provide crash logs and
limited diagnostic information to assist in improving app performance.
This section will be removed upon full App Store release.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain personal and workout data only as long as necessary to provide
services and comply with legal obligations.

Users may delete their account and associated data at any time directly
within the App via **Settings → Delete Account**. Users may also request
data deletion by contacting support at support@bellsense.app.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Children's Privacy

BellSense is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age.\
We do not knowingly collect data from children.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
