Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2026 — Last Updated: March 16, 2026

BellSense ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BellSense mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how BellSense collects, uses, and protects your data in connection with the use of the App and related hardware.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Login credentials

B. Workout & Sensor Data

  • Repetition counts
  • Exercise type
  • Session duration and timestamps
  • Performance metrics derived from motion sensor (IMU) data
  • Heart rate data (beats per minute, average, peak, and minimum) when a compatible Bluetooth heart rate monitor is connected

The App communicates with a BLE-connected sensor device to receive motion data; no data is transmitted from the App back to the hardware device. If you enable the optional "Contribute Training Data" setting, a raw binary recording of sensor readings from your session may be saved locally and uploaded to our secure cloud infrastructure (see Section 5). This feature is off by default and entirely opt-in.

C. User Feedback Data

  • Subjective workout feel ratings (1–5 scale)
  • Free-text notes provided by the user to describe or annotate sessions
  • Manual rep count corrections submitted by the user

D. Device & Diagnostic Data

  • Device model
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Performance logs

E. Analytics Data

  • Usage behavior
  • Feature interaction
  • Aggregated analytics collected via Firebase services

3. Services Used

BellSense uses Google Firebase for authentication, cloud data storage, file storage, analytics, and crash reporting. This includes Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Storage, and Crashlytics. Data may be processed on secure cloud infrastructure operated by Google.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide workout tracking functionality
  • Generate performance analytics
  • Sync workout history across devices
  • Improve rep detection, movement classification, and fatigue prediction accuracy using anonymized workout and session data
  • Build and refine personalized movement baselines and fatigue profiles stored under your account
  • Debug crashes and improve stability
  • Communicate product updates
  • Respond to user support inquiries

Session performance data (including rep metrics, velocity trends, fatigue patterns, and subjective feel ratings) may be used in aggregate and anonymized form to train and improve machine learning models that power in-app features. This data is processed under your account and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

We do not sell personal information.

5. Optional Training Data Contribution

You may opt in to contribute anonymized sensor data to help improve BellSense's exercise detection accuracy. This feature is disabled by default and can be enabled or disabled at any time via Settings → Personal Info → Contribute Training Data.

What Is Collected

When opted in, a raw binary recording of IMU (motion sensor) readings captured during your workout session is saved locally on your device and uploaded to our secure cloud infrastructure. These files contain motion sensor data only — no audio, video, location, or personal identifiers are included in the recording.

How It Is Used

Uploaded recordings are used solely to improve the accuracy of rep detection, movement classification, and exercise quality scoring. Files are stored under your account ID and are not shared with third parties for any purpose.

Opting Out

Disabling the toggle in Settings stops all future captures and uploads immediately. Previously uploaded files are deleted when you delete your account (see Section 8). To request early deletion of previously uploaded sensor files, contact us at support@bellsense.app.

6. Data Storage

Workout and account data are stored locally on your device and in secure cloud infrastructure. Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are implemented to protect your data.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers strictly for hosting, analytics, infrastructure support, or if required by law.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain personal and workout data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

Users may delete their account and all associated data at any time directly within the App via Settings → Delete Account. This includes any uploaded sensor recordings from the optional Training Data Contribution feature. Users may also request data deletion by contacting us at support@bellsense.app.

9. Beta Testing

During beta testing via TestFlight, Apple may provide crash logs and limited diagnostic information to assist in improving app performance. This section will be removed upon full App Store release.

10. Children's Privacy

BellSense is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or deletion requests:

support@bellsense.app
