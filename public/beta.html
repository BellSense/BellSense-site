Who This Is For
// This is you

Hardstyle practitioner

You train with intention. Hip hinge mechanics, power expression, full-body tension. You know what a quality rep feels like — and you want data to confirm it.

// Also you

Self-coached, program-driven

You follow structured programs. You want to know whether your effort is translating — whether your swings at rep 18 still carry the same power as rep 1.

// Not right now

Kettlebell sport / GS athletes

Different swing mechanics, different pacing strategy. BellSense is calibrated for hardstyle. GS is on the roadmap — but not yet, and we won't pretend otherwise.

// Also you

Individual workouts, your own way

You don't need a structured program to get value here. Run your own complexes, your own sets, your own schedule — BellSense tracks the metrics either way. Full programs are available if you want them. Pure session tracking is there if you don't.

What It Measures
01 — Power
Rep Power Output

Peak and average watts per rep. See how explosive each swing actually is, not just how many you did.

02 — Consistency
Velocity Retention

How much power you hold across a set. The number that tells you when you're grinding vs. still moving well.

03 — Volume
Rep Counting

Accurate rep detection with near-zero false positives. Swings, cleans, snatches — tracked by movement, not guesswork.

04 — Rhythm
Cadence

Rep tempo and rhythm across a set. Know when you're rushing, drifting, or locked in — and whether your pace held from round one to the last.

05 — Score
Session Score

A single accountability number after every session — but you're not chasing 100. You're chasing green. Green means solid training with expected fatigue factored in. A score in the training zone is the goal. Not perfection.

How It Works
Mount the sensor

The unit attaches to your kettlebell handle with adhesive and magnets — no tools, no permanent modification. Stays put during training, comes off for charging.

Train normally

Open the app, select your exercise and weight, start your session. The sensor connects over Bluetooth and begins measuring from rep one.

See your data

Real-time metrics during the session. Full breakdown after — rep-by-rep power, set summaries, velocity curves, Session Score. Your training, made legible.

The Hardware

Small enough to forget it's there. Rugged enough for chalk-dusted, sweaty training sessions. The enclosure is printed PETG — durable, lightweight, not precious.

  • Microcontroller Seeed XIAO nRF52840 Sense
  • IMU 6-axis accelerometer + gyroscope
  • Connectivity Bluetooth LE 5.0
  • Mount Adhesive + magnets — no permanent modification
  • Enclosure ~60×40×12mm PETG
  • Battery LiPo — rechargeable via USB-C
  • Platform iOS (iPhone)
